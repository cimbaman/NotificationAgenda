# NotificationAgenda
NotificationAgenda is an Android utility companion app for your existing calendar, that keeps your daily schedule exactly where you need it: in your notification tray. It ensures you never miss a meeting without needing to constantly open your calendar app.

Made by a husband for a wife. :)

## Privacy Policy
All the calendar data that the application reads is only used for display and never leaves your device.
